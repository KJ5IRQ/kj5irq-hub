Privacy Policy • KJ5IRQ & All Created Content

Effective date: 2025-08-28
Owner & Data Controller: KJ5IRQ
Contact: kj5irq@gmail.com

This policy explains how we handle information on kj5irq.radio (the “Site”) and in the Solo Dungeon Master GPT (the “GPT”), including optional dice and image actions. We practice data minimization: collect as little as possible, keep it only as long as needed, and don’t sell it.

1) What we process

a) Content you submit

  • Text you type to the GPT (character names, story choices, dice/image requests).
  • Optional metadata you share (e.g., save-state JSON).

b) Technical data (Site)

  • Standard web logs from GitHub Pages (IP address, user-agent, timestamps).

  • Dice: only the dice expression (e.g., 1d20+5) is sent to the Rolz API.
  • Images: only the prompt text is sent to OpenAI’s Images API.

2) What we don’t do

  • No selling or “sharing” of personal information (CCPA/CPRA).
  • No behavioral ads on this Site.
  • No cookies or analytics by default (we’ll add a banner and update this page if that changes).
  • Not directed to children under 13.

3) Why we process data (lawful bases)

  • Provide the service (run Site/GPT; fulfill dice/image requests).
  • Legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention, troubleshooting).
  • Consent (when you ask us to perform an action like image generation).

4) Third-party processors

  • GitHub Pages – hosting and standard server logs.
  • OpenAI (Images API) – image generation; API data not used to train by default.
  • Rolz – dice results; receives only the dice expression.

These providers have their own privacy practices; review their policies.

5) Retention

  • GPT prompts/results are transient unless you save them.
  • Action requests may be logged by providers; we don’t keep separate copies.
  • Site logs are retained by GitHub under its policies.

6) Security

  • HTTPS transport encryption.
  • Least-data principle for Action requests.
  • Admin accounts use strong authentication.
  • For any personal-data breach, we follow applicable notification duties.

7) International transfers

Data may be processed outside your region. Where required, transfers rely on safeguards such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

8) Your privacy rights

Depending on location, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict/oppose processing, or request portability (GDPR), and CPRA/CCPA rights (know, delete, correct, opt-out of sale/share, non-discrimination).

How to exercise: email kj5irq@gmail.com with “Privacy Request,” include your jurisdiction and request details.

9) Do Not Track

The Site does not track users across third-party websites and therefore does not respond to DNT signals.

10) Children’s privacy

Not directed to children under 13. If a child’s information was provided, contact us for removal.

11) Changes

We’ll update this page when practices or providers change. The effective date shows the latest version.
